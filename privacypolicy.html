Privacy Policy for Resulti

Introduction

We understand the importance of protecting your privacy online. That's why we've developed this privacy policy to inform you about the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. It applies to all services offered by Resulti.

Information Collected

We do not collect any personal information from our users. We do not track users or save their search history. Period

How we have implemented truly anonymous analytics

We do measure overall traffic numbers and some other – strictly anonymous – statistics. These stats may include the number of times our service is accessed by a certain operating system, a type of browser, a language, etc., but we don’t know anything about individual users.

About the services integrated in resulti

Some service intergrated have they own Privacy Policy. Resulti use Cloudflare as DNS management, about the number of visits in each country and the protection of the site.

Cookie

we use cookies for the good functioning of the integrated services, the site, and other. if you want to clean or block cookies, you can see the instruction here:
In Google Chrome: go to Settings > Privacy and security > Site Settings > Cookies and site data, and adjust your settings as desired.
In Mozilla Firefox: go to Options > Privacy and Security, and adjust the settings under the "Cookies and Site Data" section.
In Microsoft Edge: go to Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies, and adjust your settings as desired.
In Brave: go to Settings > Cookies and other site data, and adjust your settings as desired.
